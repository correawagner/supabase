Get started is simple. just visit [app.supabase.io](https://app.supabase.io) and sign in to start creating projects.


### Organizations and Project

After you have signed in, you can create Organizations and Projects. For example:

- Your Organisation 1
  - Your Project 1
  - YourProject 2
- Your Organisation 2
  - Your Project 3

Each project contains a full Postgres database, as well as auto-generated APIs and Auth functionality.


### Managing team members

You can invite your team members into your organizations so that you can collaborate on projects.

## Self hosting

There are various situations where you might want to self-host:

- Local development
- Migrating away

For this, Supabase provides various options under the `/emulator` folder of our [main repository](https://github.com/supabase/supabase).
