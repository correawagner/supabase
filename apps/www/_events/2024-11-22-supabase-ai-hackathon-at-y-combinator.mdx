On November 22 and 23 we're hosting an AI-focused hackathon at Y Combinator in San Francisco.

We've already seen [several 1M+ user AI applications](https://x.com/kiwicopple/status/1754777201092784209) built on Supabase and are hosting this event to support the next wave. YC has generously offered to host the event and will be there to judge projects alongside founders Copple and Ant.

### Event Schedule

#### Friday, November 22

- 5:30PM - Doors open
- 6:00PM - Welcome
- 6:15PM - Pitch ideas
- 7:15PM - Form teams and hack
- 11:59PM - Close for the evening

#### Saturday, November 23

- 9:00AM - Doors open
- 4:00PM - Code freeze
- 4:15PM - Demos
- 7:30PM - Prizes

### Jury

- YC partners
- Ant Wilson - CTO and Co-Founder at Supabse
- Paul Copplestone - CEO and Co-Founder at Supabase

### Prizes

- Supabase credits
- dinner with Supabase founders

Because we have limited capacity, we've created [a very short application](https://forms.supabase.com/yai-hackathon). We’ll keep the form open for a few weeks and let you know if you’re in by early November.

We encourage everyone to apply and hope to see you there!
